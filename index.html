RSG-Sweden

Bioinformatics · Student · Community

RSG-Sweden is one of the ISCB-SC's official group for students and researchers working/studying in the field of bioinformatics, computational biology, and related areas.

About Us

Swedish branch of ISCB Student Council's Regional Student Groups

A Community for Current and Future Bioinformaticians

The purpose of RSG-Sweden is to promote interdisciplinary research such as developments in bioinformatics happening across the country. We aim to expand bioinformatics community of Sweden through networking and knowledge exchange events.

Collaborators

Because team work makes the dream work!

Our plan is to organise networking events by collaborating with research organisations, companies, and other RSGs across Europe. Furthermore, we aim to expand a community of bioinformatics students through representatives and sub-teams in universities across Sweden.

If you plan to set up bioinformatics club/group at your university or department, please let us know and we will add you to list of our representatives. You can contact us via Slack and/or Twitter

Sub-Committees of the RSG-Sweden:

Web Committee · Finance Committee · Outreach Committee

You can read about our committees at our Slack channel. You are welcome to join any/all of the committees, regardless of your academic level and background. We look forward to working with you!

The outreach committee will be involved in organising events such as:
  • Programming Workshops
  • Networking events to bring together students from different areas such as engineering and physics
  • Bioinformatics Student Symposium

If you are interested in helping to organise any of our events, we recommend you to join our outreach committee and let us know how you would like to contribute!

Events

Keep an eye out on this section of our website for updates on our upcoming events! In the meantime, we would love to hear your ideas! If there is any event you would like us to organise and/or if you would like to organise bioinformatics-related event at your university, let us know via our Slack channel or Twitter. Tack!

Learn Bioinformatics

Inspired by the RSG-UK, this section of the website will be used to provide a collection of open source education resources (provided by Swedish researchers). It is currently under development. In the meantime, it would be great to have your ideas regarding this, let us know via Slack or Twitter.
