RSG-Sweden

Bioinformatics · Student · Community

RSG-Sweden is one of the ISCB-SC's official group for students and researchers working/studying in the field of bioinformatics, computational biology, and related areas.

About Us

Swedish branch of ISCB Student Council's RSGs

A Community for Current and Future Bioinformaticians

The purpose of RSG-Sweden is to promote developments in bioinformatics and related interdisciplinary research happening across Sweden. We aim to expand bioinformatics community event in Sweden through networking and knowledge exchange activities.

Collaborators

Because team work makes the dream work!

Our plan is to organise networking events by collaborating with research organisations, companies, and other RSGs across Europe. Furthermore, we aim to expand a community of bioinformatics students through representatives and sub-teams in universities across Sweden - therefore, to help achieve our goal, undergraduates, graduates, and/or PhD students are encouraged to contact us!

Events

Potential Events Ideas

Our plan for 2018 includes organising events such as:

  • Programming Workshops
  • Networking events to bring together biologists and engineers
  • International Women's Day: Bioinformatics Researchers

We would love to hear from you! If you have suggestions on events you would like us to organise and/or if you would like to organise an event at your institution, please contact us via Slack or Twitter. Tack!

Learn Bioinformatics

Inspired by the RSG-UK, we will utilise this section of the website to provide a collection of open source education resources. It is currently under development. In the meantime, it would be great to have your ideas regarding this, let us know via Slack or Twitter.
