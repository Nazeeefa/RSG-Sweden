RSG-Sweden

Bioinformatics · Student · Community

RSG-Sweden, affiliated with the ISCB Student Council, is the non-profit organisation for students and researchers working/studying in bioinformatics, computational biology, and related areas.

Download our flyer (PDF) to help promote RSG-Sweden!

About Us

Swedish branch of ISCB Student Council's Regional Student Groups

A Community for Current and Future Bioinformaticians

Our mission is to promote developments in bioinformatics happening across the country. By bringing students from computer science and life science backgrounds, we aim to create opportunities for cross-disclipinary collaborations.

The goal is to get more students interested in bioinformatics (computational biology, and related areas). We plan to develop bioinformatics community of Sweden through networking and knowledge exchange activities.

Collaborators

Because team work makes the dream work!

Our plan is to organise networking opportunities through collaboration with research organisations (from both academia and industry) and other RSGs across Europe. Furthermore, with the concept of RSG-Sweden's branches, we aim to expand a community of students and increase visibility of bioinformatics research in Sweden. Find out how to become our branch!

Become an affiliated branch of RSG-Sweden!

With our aim to develop bioinformatics communities across Sweden, we encourage you to join our growing network! Establishing a branch at your university gives you opportunities to connect with a wider community of students and researchers from different STEM backgrounds.

More information on this will be available soon!

However, if you are interested in setting up a branch, get in touch with us via Slack.

Join Sweden’s community of computational biologists!

To become a member, simply join us on Slack - please sign-up with your university e-mail address.

You are welcome to contribute to any/all of the committees regardless of your academic level and background. We don't ask for experience but willingness and enthusiasm to learn and grow with our amazing team! The more the merrier!

Web Committee

We are a team of developers; we maintain the website and collaborate with the outreach committte for content development. Interested in graphic designing and web development? Join our team and let's discuss ideas!

Finance Committee

We plan and maintain the budget required for RSG-Sweden's events. We are currently in search for motivated members! Join us and help develop our team!

Outreach Committee

We help organise events (includes planning fika menu!) and come up with ideas on how to promote RSG-Sweden! We are always happy to have new members! You should definitely join us if you are interested in putting together bioinformatics/-related socials and events.
