RSG-Sweden

Bioinformatics · Student · Community

RSG-Sweden, affiliated with the ISCB Student Council, is the official group for students and researchers working/studying in the field of bioinformatics, computational biology, and related areas.

About Us

Swedish branch of ISCB Student Council's Regional Student Groups

A Community for Current and Future Bioinformaticians

Our mission is to support and promote developments in bioinformatics and related interdisciplinary research happening across the country. By bringing students from computer science and life science backgrounds, we aim to create opportunities for cross-disclipinary collaborations. We hope to expand bioinformatics community of Sweden through networking and knowledge exchange activities.

Collaborators

Because team work makes the dream work!

Our plan is to organise networking opportunities through collaboration with research organisations (from both academia and industry) and other RSGs across Europe. Furthermore, we aim to expand a community of bioinformatics students through representatives at universities across Sweden.

Get in touch with us if you plan to set up bioinformatics club/group at your university or department. You can contact us via Slack and/or Twitter.

Sub-Committees of the RSG-Sweden:

You are welcome to contribute to any/all of the committees, regardless of your academic level and background. You can become a member via our Slack channel. Names of active committee members will be listed on website (with permission, of course!)

Web Committee

We are a team of developers that maintain the website and collaborate with the outreach committte for content development. We are currently looking to expand our team with graphic designer and more web developers. Join us and let us know how you would like to contribute!

Finance Committee

Our aim is to plan and maintain the budget required for RSG-Sweden's events. The committee will also work on funding opportunities including recruiting sponsors from both academia and industry. We are currently in search for motivated members! We hope you join us and help develop our team!

Outreach Committee

We are a team of event organisers, our aim is to come up with fika menu and ideas on how to bring together students and researchers! We are currently under development and looking for more members! You should definitely join us if you are interested in helping to organise bioinformatics/-related events.

Events

Keep an eye out on this section for updates on our upcoming events! In the meantime, we would love to hear your ideas! If there is something you would like us to organise and/or if you want to organise bioinformatics-related event at your university, let us know via our Slack channel or Twitter.

Learn Bioinformatics

Inspired by the RSG-UK, this section of the website will provide a collection of open source education resources (provided by Swedish researchers). It is currently under development. In the meantime, it would be great to have your ideas regarding this or if you have teaching material/blog that you would like to share for students, let us know via Slack or Twitter.
